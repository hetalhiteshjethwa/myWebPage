must visit places in victoria

otway fly treetop adventures

i visited otway fly treetop adventures on the 31st of december, 2020.

Otway Fly Treetop Adventures is a very exciting and entralling place. first of all, there is a 2km walk and when you complete the 2km walk, you get to climb a tower and see everything in the 10km radius circle around you. There is also a zipline tour (flying fox). There a 7 ziplines (getting bigger and bigger each time); 1m, 10m, 12m, 14m, 16m, 18m and 20m. There are also 2 wobbly bridges whose height are 6m and 9m. I hope that you want to visit Otway Fly Treetop Adventure any second by now that you've read all about it! I hope you have fun (if you get to go there)!

the twelve apostles

i visited the twelve apostles on the 13th of march, 2018.

The twelve apostles are 12 huge rocks (now 7) about 20.7 metres tall. There is a helicopter ride there (from above the sea) which is fun but also a bit scary and frightening. The view is absolutely incredible (trust me - it actually is wonderful). The only bad part (if I really had to name one) is that the smell stinks there. It smells like there are thousands of dead rats in the grass nearby. Anyway, to get into the topic, unfortunately, 5 of the 12 apostles fell down becuase of its huge size and heavy damaging winds. However, the other 7 managed to still stand despite the damaging wind and their size.

great ocean road

i visited great ocean road on the 13th of march, 2018.

Great Ocean Road is a very famous road in Australia because there is a lot of beaches and water around you. You can hang around on cliff-tops and do a bit of sky-diving and scuba-diving. Most people go the Great Ocean Road to have a good swim and surf. But, do not forget, most importantly, do not forget to play some beach cricket. The sand is so soft that you can even sleep through a whole night at Great Ocean Road.
